PRODUCTS

As emerging ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) regulations present new challenges, businesses are seeking effective ways to adapt. At Belto World, we recognize these challenges and are developing practical solutions. Our approach focuses on using technology to simplify the process of data collection and analysis, which traditionally requires a significant investment in sustainable finance experts. By integrating AI into our systems, we enable a streamlined process that can be managed by a smaller team of skilled professionals.

Additionally, we are utilizing blockchain technology for data storage. This not only enhances the speed and security of our operations but also distinguishes us in the market. Part of our ongoing development includes an AI-driven tool designed to automate form completion, further easing the administrative burden for our clients. Belto World is committed to offering realistic and effective tools to help businesses navigate and comply with the new ESG regulations efficiently.

Expanding on our core offerings, Belto World is not only providing an advanced ESG data collection tool but also offering expert consultancy services in AI, blockchain, and effective utilization of our ESG tool. Recognizing the complexities and resource demands of modern business technology, we offer businesses the opportunity to leverage our expertise instead of investing heavily in in-house teams. Our consultants are adept at building AI systems, implementing blockchain solutions, and ensuring clients maximize the benefits of our ESG data collection tool. This approach allows businesses to efficiently integrate cutting-edge technologies and methodologies without the overhead of hiring a full team of specialists. By partnering with Belto World, companies can streamline their operations, comply with ESG regulations, and embrace technological advancements with the guidance and support of our experienced professionals.
